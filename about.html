About us and our products

Art Salon - a store of art goods in Astrakhan for professional artists, designers, decorators, as well as those who are just trying their hand at artistic creativity.

Due to the wide range of products presented, it is equally interesting and useful for both adults and the smallest customers. The assortment of the store has already been appreciated by the Astrakhan artists for the quality of the products offered there.

The company began its activities with the aim of developing art and beauty, because through art a person manifests himself, defends his individuality and expresses his dreams and emotions.

We are the official and exclusive distributors of the Nevskaya Palitra artistic paints plant. The company began its activity with the aim of developing art and beauty, because through art a person manifests himself, defends his individuality and expresses his dreams and emotions.

Art Shop Chisinau presents several product lines: watercolors, oil, tempera, acrylic, gouache, goods for arts and crafts.We have been on the market of the Republic of Moldova for over 15 years.
